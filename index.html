I am an ADA graduate

My journey at ADA has been one of self discovery and it has literally broadened my horizon in my thinking. We have been given the tools to better ourselves as women in a male dominated space and to know that in the era that we’re living in, a feminie touch is needed for optimizing websites that’ll make a difference across the board!
